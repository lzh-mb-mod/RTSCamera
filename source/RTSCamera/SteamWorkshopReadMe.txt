[h1]RTS Camera for v1.4.6 (War Sail DLC Compatible)[/h1]

[h2]Main Features[/h2]
[list]
[*]Elevate camera when giving orders.
[*]Smoothly toggle free camera mode at any time in a battle.
[*]Control one of your soldiers and continue fighting/commanding after player character is injured in battle.
[*]Time speed control in battle. Automatic fast forward in hideout battle.
[/list]

[h2]Recommended[/h2]
[list]
[*]RTS Command: another part of the RTS mod, for commanding better. [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3747771970]Steam Workshop[/url]
[/list]

[h2]War Sails DLC Related[/h2]
[list]
[*]Added an order to let your soldiers pilot the ship with hotkey F5. This is the same feature as the Helmsman mod, but compatible with RTS.
[*]When Helmsman is installed, the order is not added for the first ship to avoid weird behavior.
[*]As Helmsman doesn't take effect for ships other than the first one, RTS will add this order when you are controlling units on the other ships.
[/list]

[h2]Dependency[/h2]
[list]
[*]Does not require ButterLib, MCM, or UIExtenderEx.
[/list]

[h2]Mod Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Incompatible with "RTS Camera Universal". That one is made by the other guy, packing my old RTS mods into one mod, conflicting with this mod. Please make sure not to use them together.[/b]
[*]If you use Realm of Thrones, please move RTS Camera above Realm of Thrones, or "text with id xxxxx does't exist" will show up.
[*]Helmsman: You don't need it if you use RTS Camera. In RTS Camera you can use F5 to toggle soldier piloting, exactly the same as Helmsman, and you will get better integration with camera related features. However, if you still use Helmsman, RTS Camera will not add F5 command for the first ship to avoid conflict and weird behavior.
[/list]

[h2]Notice[/h2]
Please clean your Modules folder and subscription list in workshop to make sure there're no duplicated mods in launcher, or the mod being loaded may not be the one you choose in launcher, and causes crash.

[h2]Save Compatibility[/h2]
This mod won't read or write anything from or into your save, so you can feel free to enable or uninstall this mod at any time.

[h2]Usage[/h2]
Please enable "RTS Camera" and "RTSCamera.CommandSystem" in launcher.

For complete usage: [url=https://github.com/lzh-mb-mod/RTSCamera/blob/master/README.md]README.md[/url]

[list]
[*]Press F10 to toggle free camera mode. The camera movement range is determined by scouting and tactics skill level.
[*]Press E to lock to a unit when the camera is not locking to one.
[*]Press E to control the unit that the camera is locking to.
[*]Press L to open config menu during battles.
[*]Set the "Timing of Control Ally After Death" option to Always to automatically control your teammate after you are down, to avoid your troops automatically charging or following AI's order.
[/list]

[h2]Hotkeys[/h2]
[list]
[*]F10 - Toggle free camera mode
[*]E - Let the camera lock to a unit, or control the unit that the camera is locking to
[*]L - Open the config menu
[*]; - Start selecting unit using mouse clicking so that you can control it by pressing E twice
[*]All the hotkeys can be changed in config menu.
[/list]

[h2]Troubleshooting[/h2]
If crash on startup, please check the following:
[list]
[*]Check Modules folder and make sure there's no RTSCamera or RTSCamera.CommandSystem. If you use mods from Steam Workshop then your Modules folder should be clean.
[*]If you are using any of the following mods from me, please make sure they are updated:
[/list]
[list]
[*]BattleMiniMap
[*]ImprovedCombatAI
[*]CinematicCamera
[/list]
